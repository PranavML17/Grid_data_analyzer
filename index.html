What is schedule vs actual load?
Every day, BESCOM submits a schedule — a 15-minute block-by-block plan of how much power it will draw from the state grid. The actual is what Bengaluru's consumers really used in real time. The gap between them is called Unscheduled Interchange (UI). A positive UI means BESCOM drew more than planned — this attracts a financial penalty. A negative UI means demand was lower than expected.
